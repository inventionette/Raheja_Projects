Geotech Services

Expert Geotechnical Services

We provide tailored geotechnical solutions to ensure your project is safe, compliant, and built on a solid foundation. Our team guides you through every geotechnical consideration, from assessments to implementation, ensuring long-term structural integrity.

Geotechnical Site Investigations

We conduct thorough site investigations to analyze soil, rock, and groundwater conditions, providing detailed reports to guide your project's design and construction phases.

Soil Testing and Analysis

Our experts perform detailed soil testing to determine load-bearing capacities, compaction levels, and other critical factors, ensuring your project adheres to engineering and safety standards.

Slope Stability and Landslide Risk Assessment

We assess slope stability and identify landslide risks, delivering practical solutions to protect your development and surrounding areas from potential hazards.

Biodiversity Offsetting and Mitigation

When impacts are unavoidable, we create biodiversity offset strategies to restore or create habitats, ensuring ecological balance is maintained.

Foundation Design and Analysis

We provide customized foundation designs based on site-specific conditions, ensuring optimal structural support for a wide range of projects, from residential buildings to large infrastructure.

Earthworks and Ground Improvement Solutions

Our team advises on efficient earthwork techniques and ground improvement methods, including soil stabilization, retaining walls, and erosion control, to enhance site conditions and minimize risks.

Geotechnical Risk Mitigation

We offer tailored strategies to mitigate geotechnical risks, such as subsidence, liquefaction, or seismic activity, ensuring your project meets safety and regulatory requirements.

Regulatory Compliance and Reporting

We guide you through local, state, and national geotechnical regulations, assisting with permit applications and ensuring compliance with all legal and industry standards.

Groundwater Monitoring and Management

We provide ongoing monitoring and management solutions for groundwater systems, safeguarding your project against flooding, seepage, and contamination issues.
