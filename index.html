Meet the Team

Navneet Raheja is a quality-focused engineer with 20 years of experience in civil engineering, project management, and design engineering, renowned for his proficiency extends to managing contractors and coordinating multi-disciplinary projects. Over the course of his career, Navneet has developed expertise in efficient project management from design to approvals across different sectors. With a significant portion of his career dedicated to serving local councils, Navneet excels in community stakeholder consultations and project development encompassing roads, drainage, bridges, and wharves. His professional services encompass project management, engineering design, technical support, and advisory, ensuring seamless project delivery from conception to completion. Navneet's commitment to quality and effective communication, coupled with his extensive experience, positions him as a trusted partner in driving successful civil engineering projects.
